## Liberty — Third-Party Notices

All third-party works are referenced by hyperlink only. Unless a page explicitly states a Creative Commons (CC) license, treat it as “All rights reserved — link only.”

- **PMC** items: reuse depends on each article’s License block (often CC BY). Verify the license on the PMC page before embedding figures or long passages.
- **Oxford Academic** page: link-only unless the article is clearly marked OA with a CC license.
- **Blogs & preprints** (NeuroLogica; Barnard preprint): assume All Rights Reserved unless the file/page states CC.
- **PubMed**: index/abstract only. Use DOI for canonical citation; page-pin in your prose where needed (e.g., Evans & Stanovich “Criticism 3,” pp. 229–231).
